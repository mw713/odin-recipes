Aglio e Olio

Description

This recipe will show you how to prepare Aglio e Olio

Ingredients

  • Dried Spaghetti
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Crushed red pepper flakes
  • Minced Fresh Parsley
  • Parmesan

Recipe

  1. Boil salted water and cook spaghetti. Set aside water before draining
  2. Heat oil and cook garlic. Add in red pepper flakes. Add reserved pasta water and boil.
  3. Add drained pasta to garlic sauce and toss. Let rest for 5 minutes. Serve with parmesan.
