Cold Somen

Description

This recipe will show you how to prepare cold somen.

Ingredients

  • Dried Somen Noodles
  • Mentsuyu
  • Chopped Scallions (Optional)
  • Grated Ginger (Optional)

Recipe

  1. Boil water and cook noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Drain noodles and rinse them under cold water.
  3. Serve with mentsuyu and optional garnishes.
